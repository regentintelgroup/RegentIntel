    Certification Programs

    Three certifications. One standard.

    Each program is self-contained, practitioner-level, and built for working professionals. Courses are delivered through our learning portal. Enrollment is by application.

    OIPT-C

    Operative Intelligence & Protective Tradecraft Certification

    13 hours / 13 CEUs. Foundational tradecraft for operators working at the intersection of protective intelligence and physical security. Built from the same doctrine that governs Regent Intel field operations.

    PIA-C

    Protective Intelligence Analyst Certification

    5.5 hours. Analytical methodology for assessing and acting on threats to a protectee, from raw signal to decision-grade product. Written for analysts supporting protective programs.

    CMI-C

    Competitive/Market Intelligence Certification

    Practitioner-level training in competitive and market intelligence collection, analysis, and reporting for corporate and commercial environments. Aligned with Federal and state regulatory requirements.
